## The Identifiers in the Dataset

* `subject` - The test subject identification.
* `activity` - The type of activity to be performed when the related measurements are executed.

## The Activity Labels in the Dataset

* `WALKING` (value `1`): The subject is walking during the test measurement.
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` (value `2`): The subject is walking up a staircase during the test measurement.
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` (value `3`): The subject was walking down a staircase during the test measurement.
* `SITTING` (value `4`): The subject is sitting during the test measurement.
* `STANDING` (value `5`): The subject is standing during the test measurement.
* `LAYING` (value `6`): The subject is laying down during the test measurement.
